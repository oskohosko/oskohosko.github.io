about me

Most of my work so far has been in Python, but as of recent I am trying to delve into some more front-end stuff as well as a little bit of back-end database work.

I've got an active subscription at Codecademy in which I'm currently doing a Machine Learning course as my main focus, with a few other smaller courses on the side to keep my skills up and repertoire wider.

Alongside golf and music, I like to dabble in a bit of Chess, studying the game and always trying to improve. At home, I like to unwind with some video games, mixology and a bit of cooking here and there.

I love a good challenge and I've designed this little webpage to keep me challenged and display my projects.
