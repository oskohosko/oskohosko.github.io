about me


I'm a recent graduate from Monash University in Melbourne with a degree in Computer Science. I have a particular interest in Software Development, especially in iOS App Development and Full-Stack Development. I'm a curious person that loves a challenge, to solve problems and want to make things that can improve and bring enjoyment to peoples' lives.

My main technical strengths and interests lie within the world of Swift, JavaScript and Python. I have recently found a love and passion for full-stack and mobile development and am always working on projects and looking to learn new things.

Outside of my studies, I'm a very keen golfer, playing every week at Rosebud Country Club. Hence, a lot of my projects revolve around golf. I believe it is the most challenging and rewarding sport out there and my projects aim to help myself and hopefully others improve and enjoy the sport just a little bit more.

Alongside golf, I'm a big concert-goer, with an ear for techno, dance and indie music. I also like to dabble in a bit of Chess, studying the game and always trying to improve. And finally, I love going to sports events, especially when the Hawks are playing at the 'G.
